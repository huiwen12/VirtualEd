We are VirtualEd.

Our Purpose

VirtualEd is a community-based tool to increase the accessibility and availability of high-quality education. We advocate for a world in which people have equal educational opportunities — the recent digitization of educational materials and content has opened the door for this goal to become a reality. With VirtualEd, any student with access to the internet can have syllabi and course materials — literature curated by leading experts in a field — at their fingertips. And better yet, students can upload their course materials and contribute to the repository, building a learning community among their fellow classmates. At VirtualEd, students are helping students learn. Together, we grow best.
